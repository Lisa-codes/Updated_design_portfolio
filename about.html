Hello! I’m Lisa Njeri. A freelance UI/UX designer with 2 years of experience, based in Nairobi.

My work includes crafting intuitive and user-focused digital experiences. My journey began in 2022, and since then, I’ve worked with brands like Synchronicity Africa, Born to Lead Mountain Adventure, and Village Market. I specialize in research-driven design, wireframing, prototyping, and high-fidelity UI, ensuring that every project aligns business goals with user needs.

I believe great design thrives on collaboration, and I work closely with developers to bridge the gap between vision and functionality. Always eager to learn, I stay updated with industry trends and refine my craft constantly. When I’m not designing, I’m out hiking, baking, reading, writing articles or seeking new creative challenges.
