Padel Tennis Mobile App

The Padel Tennis mobile app was designed to enhance the experience of playing padel tennis at the Village Market. This app allows users to effortlessly book their sessions and make payments, integrating a seamless user interface with a QR code system to easily add friends and enjoy games together. The app also simplifies the booking process, making it easier for users to explore match types and tournaments, while tracking their scores for each game played.

Client: Village Market

Sector: Recreation & Sports

Project Duration: May 2024 - June 2024 (1 month )

Role: UI/UX Designer

Foundational User Research & Competitive Analysis

To craft a seamless padel experience tailored for Village Market, we analyzed existing solutions and identified key opportunities for refinement. Our research uncovered usability gaps in leading competitors, guiding our design decisions to create a more intuitive and personalized experience.

Challenges

Lack of Dedicated Court Booking

Users struggled to **find Village Market courts** on PlayAtomic due to its generalized approach. Without a **dedicated space**, they had to sift through multiple listings, leading to confusion and frustration.

Unreliable Availability Updates

Many players reported **misleading court availability**, only to realize upon checkout that their slot was taken. The lack of **real-time updates** resulted in a broken booking experience.

Limited Game Progress Tracking

Players wanted a way to **track past matches, scores, and progress**, but existing platforms lacked a personalized history. This **gap in performance tracking** reduced engagement and motivation.

Inefficient Time Selection

Users found it **cumbersome to select available time slots**, often having to go back and forth between screens. The lack of a **clear scheduling system** made the process tedious and prone to errors.

Opportunities

Reducing Booking Confusion

While PlayAtomic excels in navigation and match discovery, its open-platform approach led to confusion when locating specific courts like Village Market. Our solution streamlines this by offering a **dedicated booking space**, minimizing uncertainty.

Personalized Booking Alerts

Users reported frustration when a previously viewed court became fully booked. To **mitigate misleading availability**, we introduced **smart notifications**, alerting players when slots fill up, ensuring they always have up-to-date information.

Autonomous Score Tracking

Unlike PlayAtomic, which lacks built-in tracking, our app empowers users with a **personalized game history**. Players can **log scores, track progress, and monitor rankings**, creating a more engaging and competitive environment.

Seamless Time Selection

Users needed a more intuitive way to book matches. We introduced a **time slot filter and a sliding calendar**, allowing players to **effortlessly select day, time, and month**, enhancing the booking flow based on real user behaviors.

Design Process

In order to empathize with the user during the design process, I crafted personas based on real life users who play the game to better craft the user experience while highlighting key user motivations and goals.

User Flow

From sign-up to booking and payments, ensuring a seamless experience, the user flows help define the problem by crafting user journey's of how the end-user would approach the application

In the Padel Tennis mobile app, the user flow begins with the Sign-Up process, where users are prompted to create an account by entering their basic information, such as name, email, and password, or by signing in using third-party authentication options like Google or Email. Once signed up, users proceed to the Home Screen, which presents available tennis courts, match schedules, and upcoming tournaments. From here, they can navigate to the Booking Section, where they select a court, choose a date and time, and confirm their booking.

Next, users are directed to the Payment Screen, where they can view the booking summary and make payments through integrated payment methods such as credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, or other available options. Upon successful payment, the user receives an Instant Notification confirming their booking details, which includes the court number, time slot, and any other relevant instructions. The app also allows users to receive notifications for upcoming matches or changes to their booking. This seamless flow ensures a user-friendly experience, from account creation to court booking and payment completion, with real-time notifications keeping the user informed throughout.

Gathering Insights

The first usability was carried out for the low-fidelity wireframes by conducting interviews with users to determine user pain points which are highlighted as shown below.



  • 25% of users found the booking process confusing.
  • 50% suggested uniform CTA buttons.
  • 25% requested more sign-up options.

Style Guide

A **consistent and cohesive design system** was essential in creating a seamless user experience. This style guide served as the foundation, ensuring **visual harmony, accessibility, and intuitive interactions** across the entire application.

  • Streamlining the booking process using time slot filters to show the particular courts available during particular times.

Challenges & Solutions

One of the primary challenges of this project was the need to deliver a fully functional app within a tight timeline. To address this, we prioritized the development of core features such as the booking system, QR code integration, and score tracking. Rapid prototyping allowed for quick iterations, ensuring that each design element was validated before moving on to the next stage.

The final product successfully streamlined the booking process, enhanced user interaction with QR code functionalities, and provided an intuitive interface for tracking game scores.

Conclusion: Elevating The Padel Tennis Experience

The Padel Tennis Mobile App was designed with a user-first approach, ensuring that every interaction was seamless, intuitive, and tailored to the needs of Village Market players. Through a combination of foundational research, competitive analysis, and user feedback, we successfully bridged the gaps left by existing solutions, providing a dedicated, hassle-free booking and tracking system.
Key Takeaways:

  • ✅ User-Centered Innovation: Addressing pain points such as court availability confusion, personalized reminders, and time-slot selection improved usability and engagement.
  • ✅ Consistency in Design: A well-defined design system ensured uniformity, accessibility, and a visually appealing interface.
  • ✅ Scalability & Future Growth: While the app is tailored for Village Market, its framework allows for future expansion, additional feature integrations, and enhanced user experiences.
By combining intuitive UX, strategic problem-solving, and a refined design system, this project exemplifies how thoughtful digital experiences can enhance real-world activities—making booking, playing, and tracking Padel Tennis matches more effortless than ever.
